Step 1: Get your PCC (Police Clearence Certificate)

The first step to your Lakshadweep adventure is to get your PCC. This certificate is mandatory for all tourists visiting the islands and ensures a smooth entry into the beautiful archipelago.

Step 2: Connect GoLaccadive

With your package booked and your PCC in hand, all that's left is to travel to Lakshadweep. Whether you choose to fly or sail, your journey to the islands will be an unforgettable experience.
